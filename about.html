Lifeline System

  • Overview
The "LifeLine: Integrated Blood Donation and Request Platform" system is designed to make donating and requesting blood easier and faster. It allows users to register as donors, request blood when they need it, and check if donors are eligible automatically. The system also keeps track of donor and recipient data safely and matches them quickly to save time. Features like blood stock tracking to help make sure blood supplies are managed well. Overall, this project aims to solve problems with blood supply and demand while making the process more efficient and accessible.
  • Significance
The " LifeLine: Integrated Blood Donation and Request Platform " system addresses common challenges in blood donation, such as delays and inefficiencies, which can mean life or death in emergencies. By simplifying and speeding up the process of matching donors with recipients, automating eligibility checks, and managing blood supplies effectively. This system helps save lives and services in delivering timely and reliable care.
